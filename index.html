BOOK SHOP

the greatest ever book shop in the world

Bookshop is an online bookstore with a mission to financially support local, independent bookstores. We believe that bookstores are essential to a healthy culture. They’re where authors can connect with readers, where we discover new writers, where children get hooked on the thrill of reading that can last a lifetime. They’re also anchors for our downtowns and communities.



list of books

Harry Potter
Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows is a rousing culmination of seven books' worth of page-turning plot

It doesn't rely solely on its previous installments to wow you. There are new characters, new dangers, and new twists to keep you guessing. It's a satisfying conclusion to a much-loved series.

Rainbow Magic
Rainbow magic book is a very attractive book for children!

The seven Rainbow Fairies are missing! Rachel and Kirsty search for one in each RAINBOW MAGIC book. Read all seven books to help rescue the fairies and bring the sparkle back to Fairyland!

Thea Stilton
the thea sisters are a lovely group of sisters who always look forward to new adventures!

To celebrate Mouseford Academy's 600th anniversary, the headmaster and teachers decide to stage a performance of William Squeakspeare's famouse play "Mouseo and Juliet"! The Thea Sisters all want the part of Juliet, but so does their rival, Ruby Flashyfur. Who will be the star of the show?

The Unicorn Quest
the unicorn quest is a book where not only there is fantasy but there are also great adventures!

Claire Martinson still worries about her older sister Sophie, who battled a mysterious illness last year. But things are back to normal as they move into Windermere Manor... until the sisters climb a strange ladder in a fireplace and enter the magical land of Arden. There, they find a world in turmoil. The four guilds of magic no longer trust each other. The beloved unicorns have gone, and terrible wraiths roam freely. Scared, the girls return home. But when Sophie vanishes, it will take all of Claire's courage to climb back up the ladder, find her sister, and uncover the unicorns' greatest secret.
